  • SASmarkdown package. The package vignettes explain how to configure your system. SAS must be installed. You can include SAS chunks in your rmarkdown document. There is no interaction between SAS and R. You can’t use SAS data from within your R chunks.

  • When you launch SAS, you can use the ‘RLANG’ option. This option was described as “pretty easy” to move between SAS and R. The option can be enabled without admin privileges, sort of.

  submit / R;
  # your R code
  endsubmit;
