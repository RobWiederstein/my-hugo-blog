Overview

This post examines Indiana Student Enrollments from 2006 through 2022.

Background

The National Center for Education Statistics (NCES) described national trends as growing through the 1950s and 1960s as a result of the “baby boom” after World War 2, declining from 1971 through 1984, increasing once again from 1985 throught 2006. Enrollments again declined between 2006 through 2011 and started growing again after 2011. The NCES predicts that enrollment changes would be relatively small through 2029.[1]
